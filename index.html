✨ Plan what matters — the rest of the time is yours ✨

This planner is yours. It's not a list of rules to follow, and it's not a clock telling you to hurry. Think of it as a way to make room for the things you care about.

Pick a few things that matter to you this week — then the rest of the time is yours to rest, play, and explore. You don't have to fill every minute. Some weeks are busy and some are slow, and both are okay.

Here's the idea:
Make Space: Set aside time for the things you want to get better at.
Protect Your Joy: Always keep room for rest, play, and the things that make you smile.
Lead Yourself: It's your day — you get to choose how it goes.

Remember: you're in charge of your own day. Be kind to yourself, try things, and enjoy the week.
